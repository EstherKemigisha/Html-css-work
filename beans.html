BEANS

Beans are a type of legume that are known for their rich nutritional content,
including proteins, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They are versatile and used in various culinary dishes around the world.
Here are a few common types of beans: Black Beans: Often used in Latin American cuisine, these beans have a dense texture and a rich flavor.
Kidney Beans: Recognized for their kidney shape and reddish color, these beans are commonly used in chili and soups.


Chickpeas (Garbanzo Beans): Key ingredient in hummus and falafel, they have a slightly nutty taste.
Lentils: Though technically different from beans, they are often grouped with them. Lentils come in various colors like green, brown, and red, and are used in soups and stews.
